Our story

It started with one pool nobody would touch.

Meridian began in 2016, when our founder inherited a backyard pool that had gone full swamp — and got quoted a drain-and-refill by every company in town. He learned the chemistry instead, brought it back without losing a drop, and realized most pools never had to get that far in the first place.

Nearly 10 years later we've rescued more than 1,900 pools across the Hill Country — and the whole company still runs on one idea: show up, stay ahead of the water, and never hand you a surprise.
