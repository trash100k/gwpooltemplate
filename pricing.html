The fine print, in plain water.

Do I need a contract?+

No. Every plan is month-to-month and your first clean is free, so you try us with zero risk. Skip a week or cancel anytime with a heads-up before your next scheduled visit.

Are chemicals really included?+

Yes — chlorine, acid, stabilizer, shock, all of it, in your monthly price and logged every visit. No surprise chemical line-items, ever.

What happens after a storm?+

Complete members get a same-week cleanup at no extra charge. Essential members can add one on demand at the à-la-carte rate.

How and when am I billed?+

One flat charge to a card on file on the 1st of the month. Annual plans are billed once up front and save you two months.

Can I pause for winter?+

Yes. Drop to bi-weekly or pause entirely in the off-season — your rate is held for whenever you come back.

Which areas do you serve?+

Austin and the surrounding Hill Country — Cedar Park, Lakeway, Bee Cave, Dripping Springs, Spicewood and nearby.

Your first clean is on us.

Start any plan and the first visit is free. No card games, no lock-in — just clear water by next week.
